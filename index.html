Cultural Corners

Palampur Embroidery

Delve into the enchanting world of embroidery and Chikankari in Palampur, where delicate threadwork intertwines with age-old craftsmanship, inviting visitors to explore the intricate beauty of traditional Indian textiles.

Ayirur Painting and Pottery

Journey to Ayirur and immerse yourself in a vibrant canvas of paintings and pottery, where every stroke and curve tells a story of cultural richness and artistic expression, offering a glimpse into the soul of a community steeped in heritage.

Pragpur Bamboo Dance

Experience the rhythmic allure of the Bamboo Dance in Pragpur, where dancers weave a tapestry of movement and tradition, inviting travelers to join in the celebration of rhythm, grace, and the enduring spirit of folk culture.
